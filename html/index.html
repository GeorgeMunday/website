Welcome to Knaresborough – a charming historic market town in the heart of North Yorkshire. Discover its rich history, stunning views, and hidden gems, from the iconic viaduct to the enchanting streets filled with character. Explore Knaresborough today!

Knaresborough is a picturesque market town nestled in North Yorkshire, England, steeped in history and charm.

Perched on the cliffs above the River Nidd, the town boasts stunning views and a rich heritage dating back to the medieval era.

Visitors are drawn to its iconic viaduct, cobbled streets, and quaint architecture, alongside attractions like Knaresborough Castle, Mother Shipton’s Cave, and the historic marketplace.

Whether exploring its vibrant array of independent shops and cafes or soaking in the tranquility of the riverside, Knaresborough offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and natural beauty for locals and tourists alike.
